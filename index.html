We hope you had a safe and happy Halloween%%=IIF(not empty(@firstName),concat(", ",@firstName),"")=%%!

Alongside all the candy and costumes, our community made the most of the holiday by raising critical funds for children in crisis through Trick-or-Treat for UNICEF.

Whether it was with click-or-treat, an online fundraiser, decorating spaces with QR codes or hosting events in your communities, so many of you got involved and we can’t thank you enough.


Over the years, Trick-or-Treat for UNICEF has raised more than $195 million in support for four generations of children. With each passing year, as more people participate, we can continue to extend life-changing assistance to children who need it the most.

Thank you again for making a difference this Halloween season — and, if you didn’t get a chance to participate this year, we’ll share reminders so you can add some meaning to your Halloweening next year!

In the meantime, explore our latest updates on children and how you and UNICEF are taking action to help around the world >>

UNICEF USA thanks Hallmark for supporting Trick-or-Treat for UNICEF in select Gold Crown stores for the entire month of October. Since 2006, this partnership has raised more than $27 million – funding health care, safe water, nutrition and education programs for children across the globe.
